Spatial and cartography.

• Cartography = It is the study of maps Maps provide visual representation of spatial relationships, allowing people to understand the arrangements of features, locations, and phenomenon of earth’s surface. Navigation, Communication, Decision Making, JOBS = There are many jobs after studying/opting Cartography as a course/subject. The IT, Data Scientist, Education, ARTS and entertainment, Government and Public Administration (Urban developer) The job prospects of Cartography professionals are promising, driven by the increasing demand for spatial Analysis, GIS, and mapping across the world. As tech advances, there is a growing need for skilled Cartographers who can leverage geospatial tools to analyze and visualize complex spatial data. Industries such as urban planning, environmental management, emergency response and business logistics rely heavily on cartographic expertise for informed decision making.
